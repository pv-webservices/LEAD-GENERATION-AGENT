FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Why TUKA Furniture Packages in Dubai?

TUKA is UAE`s #1 online furniture brand and the #1 furniture package provider with thousands of satisfied customers served to date. Why?

  • Stylish: All furniture packages and items are carefully designed, crafted and curated by a team of design experts to cater to different tastes
  • Hassle-free: Forget the old furnishing ‘drag’ that lasts multiple weeks and involves picking, buying, moving, and assembling furniture. Instead furnish your home entirely with 1-click
  • Affordable: Thanks to the direct-to-customer approach, which eliminates the traditional showroom furniture retailers, TUKA is able to offer beautiful and long-lasting furniture sourced from manufacturers that furnish 5-star hotels. For instance, a one-bedroom apartment can be furnished for 10,900 AED
  • Fast: Your new beautiful home is set-up within 1 day

How does it work?

  1. Choose your Style: All furniture packages and items are carefully designed, crafted and curated by a team of design experts to cater to different tastes (e.g., Silver Star, Bohemian Posh)
  2. Select your Furniture Package: Studio, 1-bed, 2-bed, 3-bed, 4-bed depending on your home size. Once you select the required home size all items included in the package will be displayed at the bottom of the page under “Items included in package.” Furthermore, once you click on an individual furniture piece a pop-up window with further pictures, videos, a description, the dimensions and specifications will appear.
  3. Add your Optional Item: Decoration packages, Curtains, Full Turn-Key service, etc. Once you add the Furniture Package to your cart, you will be automatically redirected to the Additional Item page. You can add additional items if desired.
  4. Receiver your Furniture: Free delivery & basic assembly within 3 – 7 business days. If you opted for the Full Turn-Key service, all that is required from you is access to your property so that your home will be ready to move-in.

I have placed an Furniture Package order, is there anything else I need to do?

  1. Payment: If you have chosen Direct Bank Transfer (DBT) as payment option, please ensure that the transfer of the funds have been initiated. Your order will only be booked and a delivery slot assigned once the payment of the outstanding amount has been credited on our bank account.
  2. Delivery date confirmation: Once the order is placed and the payment has been received from our end, our operations team will contact you via email with the earliest available slot assigned for your delivery.
  3. Move-in permit: Remember to request a move-in permit from your building management. Almost all buildings in the UAE require a move-in permit to allow the use of service lifts, access to delivery parking lots and allow delivery and installation of furniture.

Kindly ensure that a move-in permit has been issued and full access to the property is granted. If the access is denied on the day of the delivery a 500 AED rescheduling fee applies.

What is the difference between the furniture packages (e.g., Studio vs. 1-Bedroom)?

Our packages are pre-curated by our interior design experts to perfectly fit your home size. We offer studio, 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, 3-bedroom and 4-bedroom packages. The difference of the packages is the number and size of the furniture items included. For example, the 2-bedroom package includes an additional armchair in the living room and a bigger and/or different sofa size compared to the 1-bedroom, on top it has an additional furnished bedroom, etc.

Once you select the required home size all items included in the package will be displayed at the bottom of the page under “Items included in package.” Furthermore, once you click on an individual furniture piece a pop-up window with further pictures, videos, a description, the dimensions and specifications will appear.

Do I need to choose each furniture piece by myself?

No. Each furniture package is pre-curated and designed to provide a specific style (e.g., Silver Star, Bohemian Posh). Our interior design experts have already carefully designed the furniture pieces that allow to create the specific style and ambiance. You only need to choose your preferred style and select the home size. Furnishing your entire home with very good taste was never so easy: It takes literally 1-click.

We want to make a stylish home accessible to everyone and anyone: Affordable, fast and hassle-free! Everyone should be able to live in a beautiful and peaceful place that they call home.

Can I mix and match the furniture items from different Styles?

Yes. While we do not recommend mixing different items from different styles, we can assist you if that is your desire. All you need to do is to contact our team with your request (e.g., change Bed Nest from Rocky Foggy with Bed Nora from Funky Cocoon). They will inform you about the potential price difference.

Kindly keep in mind that while people often believe they have a great taste, when furnishing their own home, the opposite is the truth. Research shows that professional design experts can create homes that are considered more beautiful not only by outsiders but also the people who would have chosen different furniture for their home in the first place.

What additional packages and services are available?

Once you add the Furniture Package to your cart, you will be automatically redirected to the Additional Item page. You can add additional items if desired.

  • Living room decoration package: Decoration (artwork and decoration) that matches your selected style for those looking for a stylish upgraded interior design in the living room.
  • Bedroom decoration package: Decoration (artwork and decoration) that matches your selected style for those looking for a stylish upgraded interior design in the bedroom.
  • Curtains: Free measurement and proposal. Our team will take the measurement for the curtains and provide you a pricing accordingly.
  • Bathroom package: Essentials for your bathroom to allow a stress-free move-in.
  • Kitchen package: Essentials for your kitchen to allow a stress-free move-in.
  • Additional items that will match the style you selected.
  • Full turn-key service: You do not have the time & energy to manage the entire hassle & stress, we can take the hassle & stress away from you.

Does TUKA offer financing options for furniture packages?

Yes, TUKA provides an Easy Instalment Plan (EIP) at the check-out to make furniture packages more accessible and affordable to all our customers. Pre-requisite for an Easy Instalment Plan is a Credit Card issued by an UAE bank. For details on potential fees and conditions from your bank please refer to the information at the checkout page once you have entered your Credit Card information.

Do you have a showroom? Can I see the furniture before purchasing?

As a pure eCommerce furniture company, TUKA does not operate showrooms. You can find detailed pictures and videos of our furniture online on our website and social media accounts. Thanks to our direct-to-customer approach, which eliminates the traditional showroom furniture retailers, TUKA is able to offer beautiful and long-lasting furniture very affordable.
Our mission is to make a stylish home accessible to everyone and anyone: Affordable, fast and hassle-free! Everyone should be able to live in a beautiful and peaceful place that they call home. As such, we believe traditional and old-school furniture showrooms are outdated.

Do you offer used furniture packages?

No. All items are new from the factory.
We do not offer used furniture since used furniture almost always has stains, smells, damages and quality issues (irrespective of attention and care from pre-owner). The refurbishment costs of used furniture are 70-80% of the initial cost. As such, there is no viable business model for us. If you would like to get used furniture, we would recommend to check the 2nd hand market.

Where does your furniture come from?

We manufacture our own designed, crafted and curated furniture packages and also work with a strong network of partners. As such, we partner with upcoming brands, famous brands, retailers, manufacturers and suppliers. Our partners are global and regional companies that are known for their high-quality home furniture and are also manufacture furniture for 5-star hotels.
In case you are a business and interested in partnering with TUKA, please contact our team.

What’s your return policy?

For items purchased on our TUKANOW item store: We offer a free return for any reason at the time of the delivery, thus, we encourage you to check the items at the time of delivery. You will be refunded for the order amount excluding any delivery charges and transaction fees (if applicable). Please note that returns will not be possible after delivery.
For Furniture Packages purchased on TUKA: Kindly note that for our very affordable Furniture Packages there is no return policy.

What if an item is damaged or defective?

If an item is damaged or defective, you must contact us within 7 days of the delivery date to qualify for an exchange of the item. To help make your order perfect, we may require a photo of the original item to be exchanged.

Is the delivery included in the furniture package?

Dubai and Sharjah: Free delivery of the furniture is included in every package.
Other Emirates: For deliveries in the other Emirates (Abu Dhabi, Ajman, Fujairah, Ras Al Khaimah, and Umm Al Quwain) a delivery charge applies upon check-out.

Is the assembly included in the furniture package?

Basic assembly is included in our all our packages which means that furniture will come assembled.
However, if you do not have the time & energy to manage the entire move-in hassle & stress you can opt for our Full Turn-Key service so we take the hassle & stress away from you. We get the keys of the unit (or access to unit), receive the delivery on your behalf, unpack each single item, assemble the furniture, place and prepare the accessories (e.g., bedsheet, towels), set up the whole apartment and clean out all the boxes. Your home will be fully ready to move-in.

Once I purchase a furniture package when do I receive my furniture?

Within 3-7 business days after a successful order and payment. Once you have successfully placed the order and payment (in case of Direct Bank Transfer, funds being debited on our account), our operations team will contact you via email with the earliest available slot assigned for your delivery.
Our team will be in contact with you to discuss what is the best time for you. We will arrange the delivery of your furniture to your convenience.

Can I reschedule my delivery?

If you need to reschedule your delivery, just contact our customer support to find a new date, the following conditions apply

  • No charges if you reschedule more than 48 hours before delivery date
  • 500 AED if you reschedule less than 48 hours before delivery date

How fast can I get my furniture package in Dubai delivered?

Upon successfully placing the order and payment (in case of Direct Bank Transfer, funds being debited on our account), our operations team will contact you via email with the earliest available slot assigned for your delivery (3 – 7 business days).

How does the delivery work?

Moving into new home means hassle & stress, we are here to help you:

  1. Upon successfully placing the order and receiving the payment (in case of Direct Bank Transfer funds being debited on our account), our operations team will contact you via email with the earliest available slot assigned for your delivery (3 – 7 business days)
  2. Remember to request a move-in permit from your building management (required by most buildings in the UAE) prior to the delivery date
  3. Our operations team will deliver the furniture. Kindly note that in case of Furniture Packages for the entire property, the move-in and basic assembly can take up to a full day. As such, you would need to grant access to the property during that time.
    If you do not have the time & energy to manage the entire move-in hassle & stress you can opt for our Full Turn-Key service so we take the hassle & stress away from you. We get the keys of the unit (or access to unit), receive the delivery on your behalf, unpack each single item, assemble the furniture, place and prepare the accessories (e.g., bedsheet, towels), set up the whole apartment and clean out all the boxes. Your home will be fully ready to move-in.

Do you operate/deliver in the entire UAE?

Yes. We deliver our furniture packages in Dubai and the 6 other Emirates.
