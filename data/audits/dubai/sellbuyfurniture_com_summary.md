### Seller FAQ

How do I sell my used items to SellBuyFurniture?

Selling your used items is simple! Contact us with details and photos of what you want to sell. Our team will assess the items and provide a competitive offer. Once agreed upon, we’ll arrange for pickup and payment at your convenience.

What types of items do you buy?

We buy a wide range of items, including furniture, home appliances, kitchen appliances, and gym equipment. Whether you have a single item or a full set, we’re interested in quality, gently used goods.

How quickly can I sell my items?

We strive to make the process quick and hassle-free. After you reach out, we typically provide an offer within 24-48 hours. Pickup and payment can be arranged shortly after you accept the offer.

Do you offer free pickup for the items I’m selling?

Yes, we offer free pickup for all items you’re selling, including furniture, appliances, and gym equipment. Our team will work with you to ensure a smooth and convenient pickup process.

How do you determine the value of my items?

We evaluate your items based on their condition, brand, age, and current market demand. Our goal is to provide a fair price that reflects the true value of your goods.

Can I sell multiple types of items at once?

Absolutely! You can sell a variety of items at once, whether they are furniture, kitchen appliances, home appliances, or gym equipment. Just let us know what you have, and we’ll handle the rest.

### Buyer FAQ

How do I purchase items from SellBuyFurniture?

To purchase items, let us know what you’re looking for by specifying the type of item (e.g., sofa, refrigerator, etc.). We’ll then provide you with options, including pictures and details of available items. Once you select your preferred item, we’ll arrange for delivery or pickup.

Are the items you sell inspected for quality?

Yes, all items are thoroughly inspected to ensure they meet our high standards for quality and functionality. We only offer items that are in good condition and ready for immediate use.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we provide delivery services across Dubai and the UAE. Our team will deliver your purchased items to your location, ensuring they arrive in the same condition as when they left our facility.

Can I view the items before purchasing?

Once you select an item from the options we provide, you can schedule an appointment to view it in person before finalizing your purchase. This allows you to see the quality and condition of the item firsthand.

Do you offer warranties or guarantees on the items?

We ensure all items are in excellent working condition. While we don’t offer traditional warranties, we provide a satisfaction guarantee. If you encounter any issues upon receiving your item, please contact us immediately, and we’ll work to resolve the situation.

Can I return or exchange items after purchase?

Returns and exchanges are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you’re not satisfied with your purchase, please reach out to us within a specified period, and we’ll do our best to accommodate your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including cash, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. We aim to make the purchasing process as convenient as possible for our customers.

Do you offer discounts for bulk purchases?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk purchases. If you’re buying multiple items or furnishing an entire space, contact us for a customized quote.
