# About Us

Western Furniture LLC, established in 1992, is a member of the Arenco Group of Companies and is a leading retailer of high quality Italian brand furniture for home environment, outdoor spaces and furniture for offices and public areas.

Based in the UAE with showrooms in Dubai and Abu Dhabi, the company, driven by a pioneering vision has successfully attracted eminent Italian furniture brands such as Natuzzi Italia, Natuzzi Editions, Calligaris, Reflex Angelo, Bonaldo, Miniforms, Kristalia, Adriani & Rossi, Tomasella, and Zanette, Tonin Casa and Gamma International along with German brand Draenert under their portfolio.

Western Furniture Shops in Dubai offers a unique collection of bedroom sets, living room furniture such as dining tables, chairs, bars, bar-stools, sofas, ottomans, cabinets, wall units, lighting, dining tables, along with home décor accessories to suit the budget and taste of every customer. It offers contemporary furniture designs for every space of the house ensuring a completely coordinated furniture solution for your dream home.

The brands represented by Western Furniture offer distinctive home décor solutions that represent a connection with the past, present, and future and are characterized by exceptional quality and timeless elegance.
